Vancouver Art Book Fair#

  • What: Annual celebration of books as art, zines to monographs
  • Vibe: Meet creators, connect with the zine community
  • Find it: vancouverartbookfair.com

Zine Harvest#

  • What: Semi-annual zine fair at Sun Wah Centre
  • Vibe: Meet creators, discover new zines
  • Find it: @zine.harvest

Venues & Spaces#

Moniker Press#

  • What: Risograph publishing studio
  • Vibe: Collaborative, works with artists on books/zines
  • Find it: monikerpress.ca / @monikerpress

Steph Ford Studio#

  • What: One-woman risograph print studio
  • Workshops: Zine-making + riso printing
  • Find it: stephfordstudio.com

Moniker Press Riso 101 Workshops#

  • What: Online workshops for riso printing beginners
  • Vibe: $60 sliding scale, 3-3.5 hours, no prior knowledge needed
  • Find it: monikerpress.ca/workshops

Lucky's Books and Comics#

  • What: Comics shop that often stocks local zines
  • Vibe: Support local zine makers
  • Find it: luckys.ca | 3128 Main St

Pulp Fiction Books#

  • What: Independent bookstore with zine section
  • Vibe: Curated selection of local and indie publications
  • Find it: pulpfictionbooksvancouver.com | Main St & Commercial Dr locations
